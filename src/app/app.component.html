Our Story

The couple met year 2017 in a university in CDO where they both graduated. Their first encounter was during the campus ministry, sharing the gospel to random students and do ministry together in a youth ministry. Norvin and Grace have the same mission and love for Jesus. They both have the gift of music and even in their differences they both have the same sense of humor. Norvin planned marrying Grace from the very start of the story. And now it became realized to really make it happen for his love, commitment and for the glory of God.
